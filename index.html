A civic-minded radio club.

Ham radio connects neighbors during calm days and emergencies alike. Our members build skills in electronics, antennas, and on-air etiquette — then put them to work through public service, mentorship, and friendly conversation on the bands.

Licensed members
Technician through Extra — all bands, all modes.
Newcomers
